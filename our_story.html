Our Story

Welcome Leasing Lao wants to be a well-known company in Laos

Star brand ‘Welcome’ in Asian retail financing market

Since its business start in Oct.2016, Welcome Leasing Lao has been dedicated to providing quality leasing service to the people of Laos. It is already proud of being one of the leading leasing companies in Laos, which could be possible despite of short operating period with enthusiastic efforts for better service from employees as well as the full support from Welcome Financial Group renowned for sufficient experience in the consumer finance sector and financing methodology led by cutting-edgy ICT.

Though its business is currently limited to leasing only, Welcome Leasing Lao will never neglect to develop the financial solutions that will facilitate the various needs of customers, which will take it to achieve its ultimate goal of 'the best multi-finance company in Laos’.

History of the Welcome Leasing Lao

  • 2016.10
